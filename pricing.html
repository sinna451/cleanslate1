Frequently Asked Questions

What happens when I hit the free limit? ▼
The tool will show a message when you've used your 3 daily images. Your limit resets every 24 hours. Upgrade to Pro for 50 images/day.
Can I cancel my Pro subscription anytime? ▼
Yes, absolutely. You can cancel from your account settings at any time. You'll keep Pro access until the end of your billing period.
Is my payment info secure? ▼
All payments are processed by Stripe, a PCI-compliant payment processor trusted by millions of businesses. We never store your card details.
Do my images get stored on your servers? ▼
Never. All image processing happens entirely in your browser. Images are not uploaded to any server — not even ours. Only your usage count and account info are stored.
What is batch processing? ▼
Batch processing lets Pro users upload and clean up to 50 images at the same time in one go, instead of one image at a time.
