Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about CleanSlate.

General
What is CleanSlate?▼
CleanSlate is a free browser-based tool that removes metadata and AI fingerprints from your images. It strips EXIF, GPS, XMP, C2PA credentials, Stable Diffusion parameters, and AI generator signatures — all without uploading your images to any server.
Are my images uploaded to your servers?▼
Never. All processing happens entirely in your browser using the HTML5 Canvas API. Your images never leave your device. Only your account info and usage count are stored in our database.
What types of metadata does CleanSlate remove?▼
CleanSlate removes: EXIF data (camera model, settings, timestamps), GPS location data, XMP and IPTC metadata, C2PA content credentials, Stable Diffusion generation parameters, AI generator signatures, and modifies pixel fingerprints to change the image hash.
What image formats are supported?▼
CleanSlate supports JPG/JPEG, PNG, WebP, and AVIF as input formats. You can export cleaned images as JPEG, PNG, or WebP.
Will cleaning my image reduce quality?▼
Minimal quality loss occurs since images are re-encoded through the browser canvas. You can control output quality (75%–95%) in the settings strip. The pixel modification adds imperceptible noise that changes the image fingerprint without visible quality loss.
Account & Limits
How many images can I clean for free?▼
Free accounts can clean 3 images per day. Your limit resets every 24 hours. Upgrade to Pro for 50 images/day and batch processing.
Do I need an account to use CleanSlate?▼
No account is needed to use the free tier. However, creating a free account lets us track your daily usage accurately and makes it easy to upgrade to Pro whenever you need more.
When does my daily limit reset?▼
Your daily limit resets every day at midnight UTC. Free users get 3 images/day and Pro users get 50 images/day.
